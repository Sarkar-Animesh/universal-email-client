Place icon PNGs here:

  icon-192.png             192×192
  icon-512.png             512×512
  icon-maskable-512.png    512×512, ≥10% safe area on each side for mask

Quick-generate from an SVG:
  npx pwa-asset-generator logo.svg ./icons --background "#0b1220" --maskable

The PWA installability check (Lighthouse) requires at least the 192 and 512 icons.
